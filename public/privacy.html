Privacy Policy for Obi V1

Last updated: 19 November 2025

Obi V1 (“the App”), developed by MGW Performance Systems, provides diagnostic tools, vehicle data insights, workshop utilities, and parts reference information for automotive enthusiasts.

1. Information We Collect

The App does not collect personal information unless voluntarily provided. The App may process the following non-personal device data when connected to an OBD-II adapter:

  • Live ECU data (RPM, boost, temperatures, sensor values)
  • Diagnostic trouble codes (DTCs)
  • Freeze-frame and readiness information
  • Adapter connection details (Bluetooth name & address)

This data is processed locally on your device only and is never transmitted to external servers without your consent.

2. How We Use Data

Data collected via OBD-II is used exclusively for:

  • Displaying real-time vehicle information
  • Performing diagnostics
  • Providing workshop specifications
  • Improving user experience and app performance

No data is sold, shared, or used for advertising.

3. Third-Party Services

Obi V1 may interact with:

  • Bluetooth services on your device
  • Optional online services (e.g., parts search or engine data packs)

All third-party usage complies with Google Play policies.

4. Data Storage & Security

All data is stored locally on your device unless cloud features are enabled by the user. We implement reasonable technical measures to ensure your data remains secure.

6. Children’s Privacy

The App is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.
